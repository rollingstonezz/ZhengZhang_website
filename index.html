About me

I am a last year PhD candidate in computer science at Emory University. My research focuses on developing innovative machine learning techniques for graph-structured data and large language models. I have expertise in areas including graph representation learning, anomaly detection, and curriculum learning.

My academic work has led to multiple publications at top conferences like NeurIPS and ICDM, including receiving the Best Paper Award at ICDM 2022. I also have strong industry experience, with internships at Amazon, TikTok, and Zhuoshi quantitative trading firm.
